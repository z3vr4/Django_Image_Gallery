About PhotoApp

PhotoApp is a Django App I've built as a way to practice and familiarize myself with the Django framework, and where I started my journey into web development.

So, what does PhotoApp do?

Basically, its a user oriented image gallery, where people may create profiles, upload images and share their thoughts on said images.

This sort of web app was what I chose to start with since in essence, it basically replicates what a lot of other platforms allow users to do.
